  • Prep Time: 1 hrs 25 mins
  • Cook Time: 1 hrs 15 mins
  • Adicional Time: 1 hrs 25 mins
  • Total Time: 2 hrs 50 mins
  • Servings: 8
  • Yield: 1 9x13-inch lasagna

Ingredients

  • Meat: 1lb ground meat (1/2 ground pork, 1/2 ground beef)
  • Onion: A diced onion cooked until translucent with the ground meat
  • Canned tomatoes: Can of tomato sauce and a can of crushed tomatoes.
  • Fresh herbs: chop two tablespoons of parsley and crush one clove of garlic.
  • Sugar: Dash of sugar to balance the acidity from the tomatoes.
  • Spices and seasonings: Seasoned with dried basil, drid oregano, salt, and black pepper.
  • Noodles: Uncooked or oven-ready noodles.
  • Cheese: Cottage cheese, Permesan, shredded mozzarella
  • Eggs: To make the cheese layer extra creamy and act as a binding agent.
